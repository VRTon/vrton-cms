# VRTon 2026 Volunteering and Collaboration Terms

Learn the conditions to be part of the VRTon team

## VRTon Volunteering Terms

The objective of these terms is to establish a clear and transparent framework for those who wish to collaborate, either individually or representing communities.

Responsibilities of volunteer communities\n\n- Designate at least one official representative to maintain communication with the VRTon staff.\n- Meet the deadlines and requirements agreed upon for proposed activities or contributions.\n- Ensure that their contributions comply with the Terms and Conditions, the Code of Conduct, and applicable laws.\n"
